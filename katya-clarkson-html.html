Katya Clarkson - Age, Family, Bio

Best known as the daughter of Top Gear television host Jeremy Clarkson. 

Before Fame

Before her parents were married in 1993, her father was previously briefly married to Alexandra James in 1989. 

Trivia

A passionate photographer, she has showcased her work on her own website and her katyaclarkson Instagram account. 

Family Life

Her father married her mother, Frances Cain, in 1993. She has two siblings, a sister named Emily and a brother named Finlo. 

Associated With

Her grandfather on her mother's side is war hero and Victoria Cross recipient Robert Henry Cain.
