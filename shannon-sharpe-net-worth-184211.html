Shannon Sharpe Net Worth

Shannon Sharpe is a former NFL tight end who was born on June 26, 1968 in Chicago, IL. He was named to eight Pro Bowls in his 13-year career and won a total of three Super Bowl Championships, two with the Denver Broncos and one with the Baltimore Ravens. He is considered one of the greatest tight ends of all time.
