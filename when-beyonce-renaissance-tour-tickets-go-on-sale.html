When do Beyonc's Renaissance tour tickets go on sale?

BEYONCÉ sent her fans into a frenzy with news that she's hitting the road again.

The GRAMMY Award winner is embarking on another tour to promote her seventh album Renaissance.

When do Beyoncé's Renaissance tour tickets go on sale?

Ticket sales for Beyoncé's Renaissance tour go live on Monday, February 6.

In anticipation of the high ticket demand outweighing the number of seats available, Ticketmaster has already taken certain measures to organize the volume of purchases.

"It is expected that many interested fans may not be able to get tickets because demand drastically exceeds supply," they stated in a press release.

How can I get Beyoncé tour presale tickets?

Admission for Beyoncé's Renaissance tour has been separated into three different presales.

All three of the below-mentioned purchasing methods fall under the Verified Fan sales category and include:

  • The BeyHive presale
  • The Citi Presale, open to CitiBank cardholders
  • Verizon Up Presale, open to Verizon account holders

In order to take part in the BeyHive presale, the public has to head over to the Break My Soul singer's official website to sign up to be a member.

To participate in any of the three presales, fans have to follow the step-by-step instructions provided by Beyoncé's LiveNation artist page.
