Anthony Stinson Accident: What Happened To Him As GoFundMe Raises More Than $1,200 Following Police

For a 13-year-old boy to lose his life, and to do that through an accident that is a result of someone’s negligence, is a very painful one. Anthony Stinson’s accident details have been widely searched, as people are interested in knowing what happened to the boy. Meanwhile, in the wake of his sad death, GoFundMe has raised about $1,200 in support.

In this article, we are going to delve into the details of Anthony Stinson’s accident and what happened afterward. If you want to know more about how the 13-year-old boy lost his life, this is the article to follow.

Anthony Stinson Accident: What Happened To Him?

On Saturday, September 9, 2023, in Shirley, a Suffolk County Patrol vehicle struck Anthony Stinson, a 13-year-old child. The teen’s mother, Claudia, then told News12 that her son died on Monday as a result of his injuries.

Stephanie Byrnes, who met Anthony Stinson when they went camping together a few years ago, started a GoFundMe appeal as soon as she learned of the teenager’s passing. The goal of the campaign is to reach $2,000, and as of the time of writing, 32 donations have helped raise more than $1,000.

After being struck by a police patrol car on Saturday evening while riding his bicycle, Stinson was in critical condition. According to News 12, the collision allegedly happened at the intersection of Adobe Drive and William Floyd Parkway.

Investigations Into Anthony Stinson Accident

Anthony Stinson’s accident that led to his sad death is currently being investigated. The police officer who was operating the patrol car had the lights and siren on, according to Suffolk County officials who clarified the incident. According to reports, they were en route to the emergency call. Detectives also alleged that the police officer ended up hitting three other automobiles as they swerved and sought to save the kid.

As per CBS, Johnny Ray, a witness, told the publication that there were “constant accidents” in the area. “The police officer wasn’t in the vehicle … By the time I went from my front door to her car, she was already out holding him,” they said. A full investigation into the collision is ongoing, and more details are needed.

GoFundMe In Support Of Anthony Stinson Funeral

Anthony Stinson’s accident that led to his death is being investigated. In the meantime, his funeral is being planned, and a GoFundMe is being organized to raise funds for it. The GoFundMe page was started to raise money for the deceased teen’s funeral costs. In the post, Byrnes praised the young man and requested readers to give $30 in honor of his football jersey number, 30.

Anthony Stinson was noted as being eager to learn new things. Stephanie reported that Claudia, Anthony’s mother, made the announcement that her son Anthony, 13, would have his organs donated.

Announcing his sad passing, one member of the team wrote, “13-year-old boy from Suffolk, NY, was struck by a police car responding to an emergency while riding his bike on September 9th evening. The collision left Anthony in Stony Brook Children’s Hospital, paralyzed and fighting for his life. It is with great sadness to say Anthony has passed away today.”
