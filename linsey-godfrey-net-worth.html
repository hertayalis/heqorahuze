Then she was diagnosed with cancer, to be precise, Hodkin’s lymphoma. Consequently, she had to undergo treatment and made a seven-month career break.

Later, she played the role of Eliza in the feature film “The Assignment” (2010). Following this, she made appearances in episodes of the television series “The Suite Life on Deck” (2010), “Wizards of Waverly Place” (2011) and “CSI: Miami” (2011). All contributed to her net worth

Since 2012, she has had a regular role in the television soap opera “The Bold and the Beautiful” created by William J. Bell and Lee Phillip Bell. For the role of Caroline Spencer Forrester, the actress has been twice nominated for the Daytime Emmy Award.
