Taylor Swift Shines in a Gold Sequin Dress at the 2024 Grammys After-Party

Taylor Swift, who won her fourth Grammy for album of the year in 2024, celebrated in style. The singer wore a gorgeous gold sequin dress to her record label’s after-party, where she interacted with friends and fellow artists.

A Sparkly Outfit Change

Swift, who wore a couture Schiaparelli gown with black opera gloves and a watch choker on the red carpet, changed into a more joyful ensemble for the after-party.

She wore a gold sequin dress from Valentino’s Resort 2024 collection, with spaghetti straps, a deep-V neckline, and a sheer overlay. Swift looked stunning in the $18,000 dress as she posed for photos and hugged her friends.

She wore the dress with white strappy shoes and maintained her usual red lip and side braid. She also wore a ring that said “TS,” which some fans interpreted as a hint at her impending re-recording of Reputation (Taylor’s Version).

A Night to Remember

Swift had a lot to celebrate at the after-party, having just revealed her surprise eleventh studio album, The Tortured Poets Department, during her acceptance speech for Best Pop Vocal Album for Midnights.

Swift’s love of poetry and her previous romances have inspired the album, which will be released on April 19, 2024. Swift also won the coveted Album of the Year award for Midnights, making her the first female artist to receive the honor four times.

She has won Fearless (Taylor’s Version) in 2010, 1989 in 2016, and Folklore in 2021. She praised her fans, collaborators, and boyfriend, Travis Kelce, who joined her during the ceremony.

Swift was seen having a great time with her friends at the after-party, which included Ed Sheeran, Sabrina Carpenter, Ice Spice, and Caroline Polachek. She also met with her erstwhile opponent Kanye West, who congratulated her on her victory and apologized for interrupting her speech at the 2009 VMAs.
