Dionne Warwick is an iconic singer and songwriter born on December 12, 1940 in East Orange, NJ. She is best known for her partnership with Burt Bacharach, as well as her role as a spokesperson for the Psychic Friends Network. Her career has spanned over five decades, and she has released over 70 albums and singles.
