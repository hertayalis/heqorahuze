Beth Mowin’s Biography (Age)

Beth Mowins was born on May 26, 1967 in Syracuse, New York as the only sister of 3 brothers. The Mowins family is known for having a flair for sports. While her father worked as a basketball coach, her mother followed ice hockey very closely. Out of a love of sports, which came from both parents, the family watched different sports all the time and maybe that ignited the light of sports in Beth.
