Troye Sivan Net Worth: How Much Is Troye Sivan Worth?

Troye Sivan net worth-Australian singer, former YouTuber and actor, Troye Sivan Mellet was born on June 5, 1995.

Sivan was born in Johannesburg, South Africa to Laurelle Mellet and Shaun Mellet. He shares the same parents with his three siblings; Tyde Levi, Steele Mellet and Sage Mellet.

Although he does not consider himself to be religious, he was reared in an Orthodox Jewish household. His mother converted to Judaism, and his father was born into a Lithuanian-Jewish Jewish family.

Sivan attended the exclusive Modern Orthodox school Carmel School up until 2009, when he began his distance learning. He adopted Sivan as his last name, which is a Hebrew month.

Troye Sivan career

Sivan has over 7.3 million followers and over 1.3 billion cumulative views as of 2021. Sivan is a well-known YouTube celebrity.

Sivan got a contract with EMI Australia in 2013 after becoming well-known as a singer on YouTube and in Australian talent shows. In 2014, he released TRXYE, his third extended play, which reached at number five on the US Billboard 200.

The album’s debut track, “Happy Little Pill,” peaked at number ten in Australia. He released his first studio album, Blue Neighborhood, in 2015, after his fourth extended play, Wild.

“Youth,” the album’s debut song, is Sivan’s first track to peak in the top 40 of the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

In Australia and on the Billboard 200 list, his second studio album, Bloom (2018), peaked at number three and number four, respectively.

“My My My!” the album’s lead track, rose to the top of the Billboard Dance Club Songs list for the second time for Sivan. His EP In a Dream was published in 2020.

Sivan is an actor who acted as the younger Wolverine in X-Men Origins: Wolverine (2009) and the Spud trilogy’s main character.

In 2017, he received the GLAAD Stephen F. Kolzak Award at the youngest age ever. For the song “Revelation” from the movie Boy Erased, in which he also had a supporting acting role, he was nominated for a Golden Globe in 2018. Since then, he has appeared in the movies Three Months (2022) and The Idol (2023).

Sivan represented Valentino’s Spring/Summer 2018 line in 2018. He served as a spokesperson for MAC’s Viva Glam campaign in 2019.

He also participated in the #MyCalvins and #CK50 campaigns for Calvin Klein that year. He has served as a Cartier ambassador since 2020.

The Sivan and Uniqlo-designed capsule t-shirt line was made available in February 2021. He appeared as a model in the third installment of Rihanna’s Savage X Fenty Show on September 24, 2021.

He participated in Beyoncé’s Ivy Park’s early 2022 Adidas capsule collection campaign. Since 2022, he has served as a YSL Beauty ambassador.

Lorde, Taylor Swift, and Amy Winehouse are some of Sivan’s favorite musicians. Frank Ocean and Michael Jackson are two other influences on him.

As his music video for “My My My!” was obviously impacted by the one for “Dirrty” (2002), Sivan also credits Ariana Grande and Christina Aguilera as his musical influences.

The term “layered electropop with constant tinges of EDM” has been used to describe Sivan’s music. His baritone voice has drawn significant appreciation, and Lorde and Taylor Swift have been compared to him and his work.

Sivan was ranked number 34 in Rolling Stone Australia’s “50 Greatest Australian Artists of All Time” issue published in December 2020.
