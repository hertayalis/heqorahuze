21 Savage | Career

The rapper’s storytelling ability in his songs caught the attention of producers, and in 2015 he released his mixtape “The Slaughter Tape.”

He released another mixtape, ” Slaughter King,” later that year.

The rapper became well known and leaped forward in music with his “Savage Mode,” which peaked on Billboard’s 200 charts.

It rose to number 24, and his song “X” became his first platinum record.

His album “Issa Album” peaked at Billboard 200’s number 2 and sold 77,000 units during its first week of release.

He faced controversy in 2018 with his movement called “Guns Down, Paintballs Up,” as it ignited homicide and crimes. The campaign backfired.

“I Am > I was” album became number 1 on Billboard, and it featured artists like Post Malone and Travis Scott.

21 Savage has won Grammy for Best Rap Song (2020), an MTV Video Music Award for Song of the Year (2018), and Billboard Music Awards for Top Rap Song (2018).
