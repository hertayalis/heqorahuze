Missing Mark Edward Lowell Found Dead In White Bear Township Lake

The Ramsey County sheriff’s office reports that a body has been discovered in a lake in White Bear Township, where a man went missing on Wednesday night.

According to Ramsey County Undersheriff Mike Martin, a body was found in Bald Eagle Lake on Friday at around 6 o’clock.

The medical examiner’s office in Ramsey County will reveal the victim’s identify, according to Martin.

Mark Edward Lowell, 62, was reported missing in the lake off U.S. 61 at around 8 p.m. on Wednesday, according to authorities.
