Biography

He is known for playing as a part of the Olimpia club along with the Honduran national team. As a member of the Honduras national team, he can be seen in relation to other players including Maynor Figueroa and Emilio Izaguirre. With a passion for the sport at a young age, he first began playing for the youth club team of Olimpia starting in 1999. After ten years with the team, he started his senior career for Olimpia. He earned more recognition after 2008 when he began playing for the Honduras Under 17 national team.
