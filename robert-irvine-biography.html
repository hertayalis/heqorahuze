Robert Irvine Bio (Wiki)

Who is Robert Irvine?

Robert Irvine is an English Celebrity Chef. He appeared in and hosted many food-based reality shows in America including ‘Dinner: Impossible’, ‘Worst Cooks in America, and ‘And Restaurant: Impossible. and so on.

Robert Irvine is also known for his talk show, ‘The Robert Irvine Show.

Robert Irvine: Career, Profession

Robert initiated his cooking career in his early teenage period. After getting enlisted in the Royal Navy, he completed culinary training. He then got the opportunity to serve aboard ‘Her Majesty’s Royal Yacht Britannia’.

His TV career as a celebrity chef, all started when he was featured in a TV reality-based show called “Fit for a King”.

This show changed its name to “Dinner: Impossible” where he faced various culinary challenges.

Robert Irvine got fame after appearing on the show “Restaurant: Impossible” as the host. Later he also hosted various food shows on TV.

He has many restaurants in the United States and is the first celebrity chef to open a restaurant in the Pentagon. Presently, he hosts his daytime talk show, “The Robert Irvine Show”.

Robert Irvine has won numerous awards and honors like “United States Navy Honorary Chief Petty Officer”, “Ambassador of the Culinary Institute of America” and so on.

Discovery+ announced a new series starring Irvine called The Globe in June 2021.
