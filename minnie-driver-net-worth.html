Minnie Driver is an English actress. She had her career beginnings in the 1990s. After her initial roles in the films Circle of Friends and GoldenEye, among others, she had her breakthrough in the movie Good Will Hunting asSkylar Satenstein. For her role here, she got an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress. She next voiced Jane Porter in the animated film Tarzan. In the 2000s, she had roles in the films Ella Enchanted, The Phantom of the Opera, and Take. She then starred on the TV series The Riches, for which she got a Golden Globe nomination. In the 2010s, she starred on the short-lived series About a Boy. As of 2016, she plays the lead on the ABC sitcom Speechless.
